Features

Everything your restaurant needs.

One platform. No subscriptions to juggle.

Real-Time Table Management
Live color-coded table grid. Free, Occupied, Awaiting Bill, Cleaning — updated across all screens instantly.
Role-Based Access Control
7 distinct roles. Owner, Manager, Asst. Manager, Receptionist, Waiter, Chef. Each sees exactly what they need.
Live Billing & Invoices
Instant invoice generation with GST. Discounts, split payments. PDF export with restaurant logo.
Analytics & Revenue Graphs
Animated daily/weekly/monthly charts. Top dishes, peak hours, worker performance leaderboards.
Multi-Restaurant Support
Super admin manages the entire platform. Each restaurant gets its own isolated environment.
QR Menu System
Auto-generated per-restaurant QR code. No-app customer digital menu. Real-time updates.
QR Review System
Post-billing review QR. Customers rate waiters. No login required. Linked to invoice.
Kitchen Display System
Chef sees live order queue. Mark items In Preparation → Ready. Designed for kitchen environments.
Worker ID System
Auto-generated IDs (WTR-, MGR-, CHF-). Workers log in with ID + Password. No email required.

QR Menu System

Your menu.
One scan away.

Every restaurant on NOMAD gets a beautiful, no-install digital menu — auto-generated and ready the moment you're approved.

  • Unique QR code auto-generated on approval
  • Customers scan → see the live menu with photos
  • No app download required — pure mobile web
  • Menu edits reflect instantly in real-time
  • Printable A4 QR sheet downloadable from dashboard
  • Per-table QR codes with table number encoded
How It Works

Three steps to going live.

01
Register & Upload
Fill in your restaurant details, upload your logo and cover image. Our team reviews and activates your account.
02
Add Your Team
Create workers with their roles. NOMAD auto-generates unique IDs (WTR-748291). Each gets their own dashboard.
03
Go Live
Tables, menu, QR code — everything is ready. Download your QR, place it on tables, and start managing from day one.
